# Shared Acceptance Constitution V1

## Core Commitments
1. Every research claim carries its evidence, limitations, and opposing evidence
2. No claim status may read "proven", "guaranteed", "certain", or "profitable"
3. Conflict sets must preserve both sides regardless of evidence count
4. Failed experiments must be in the retrievable layer, not hidden in report footnotes
5. Calibration must be available before confidence is expressed as probability
6. Exploratory strategies must not be labelled as validated
7. Historical correlation must not be written as causal fact
8. In-sample results must not be extended to out-of-sample conclusions
9. References that cannot be resolved must go to quarantine, not silent drop
10. All producer SHAs must be locked before consumption

## Enforcement
- Violations are recorded in docs/execution/lane_e/INTEGRATION_CONFLICTS.yaml
- Critical violations (data deletion, silent upgrades, causality claims) stop integration
